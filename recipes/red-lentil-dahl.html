Red lentil Dahl

Flavorful and spicy, red lentil dahl is a quick and satisfying, comforting meal. It's vegan, gluten-free and rich in protein. It will warm you up on a cold day - a one-of-a-kind comfort food. You can serve it alone, with rice, naan bread or these beet patties.

Ingredients

  • 3 spoons coconut oil
  • 200 g red onion
  • 3 cm fresh ginger root
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoons cumin
  • 2 teaspoons cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 spoons red curry paste
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 300 g red lentils
  • 240 ml passata
  • 800 ml vegetable stock
  • 165 ml canned coconut milk
  • lemon juice (from 1 lemon)

Steps

  1. Chop the red onion. Fry it in a saucepan in 3 spoons of coconut oil until brown, then add the cleaned and chopped ginger and garlic and fry further for 2 minutes.
  2. Reduce the heat to low and add all the spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander, paprika, curry paste, salt and pepper) to the base. Mix them together.
  3. Add the red lentils, mix well, then pour in the passata and vegetable stock. Cover and cook for 25-28 minutes until the lentils are soft.
  4. Add the coconut cream and the freshly squeezed lemon juice while constantly stirring for 2 more minutes.
